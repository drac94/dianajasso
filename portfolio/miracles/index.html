MIRACLES Candle Company

The visual identity and packaging design of a new candle brand, MILAGROS, was developed. A brand of handmade candles with innate natural fragrances. The design and concept is created with graphics inspired by the folklore of Latin peoples, where candles are used as offerings for miraculous requests. An idea with a touch of mysticism and modernity.
